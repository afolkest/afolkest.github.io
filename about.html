About

Where can spacetime tear apart? Is it only at the Big Bang and inside black holes, or is the phenomenon more widespread? This is one of the central questions I worked on during my PhD at MIT and my postdoc at UC Davis. More broadly, I specialized on the connection between gravity and quantum information theory. Before that I've been here and there. I was a Fulbright scholar during my masters degree, and I also spent at year at CERN working on particle detectors, realizing that the screwdriver is better entrusted to someone else. These days I work on using LLMs to build powertools for scientists at the startup Undermind.
