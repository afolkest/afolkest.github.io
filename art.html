Electrogenesis

I made this image simulating the electric field around thin sheets of metal, followed by smearing white noise along the field lines (so-called line integral convolution). You can see that the field lines always meet the metal shapes perpendicularly, which is a characteristic feature of metals. This image and the ones below were made with Elliptica, which is an open source drawing program for painting with the laws of physics that I am making.
